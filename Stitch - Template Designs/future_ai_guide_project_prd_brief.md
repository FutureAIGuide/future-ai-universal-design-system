# Project Brief: Future AI Guide (OS Intelligence)

## 01 — Mission & Vision
**Product Vision:** To occupy the evidence-based gap between AI maximalism and catastrophism by making AI claims admissible through deep forensics and technical oversight.
**Brand Doctrine:** "Controlled Menace" — Direct, evidence-aware, visually disciplined, and technically literate.
**Primary Objective:** Establish the authoritative intelligence network for AI resource discovery and investigative journalism.

---

## 02 — Product Architecture (The "OS")
The platform operates as a multi-layered Intelligence OS, divided into three core functional areas:

### A. The Resource Hub (Platform)
*   **AI Directory:** A high-density, searchable repository of validated neural architectures, LLMs, and agent frameworks.
*   **Intelligence Detail:** Technical specs (architecture, latency, throughput) mapped against operational domains (Safety, Compliance, Forensics).
*   **Knowledge Base:** Structured learning roadmaps and technical documentation for AI implementers.

### B. The Investigative Arm (Editorial)
*   **Case File Repository:** A dense archive of investigative journalism into AI behavior, security breaches, and corporate accountability.
*   **Evidence Matrices:** Structured blocks for multi-source citation and verification status.
*   **Verdict Engine:** Semantic signaling (Verified, Breaking, Under Review) to categorize the urgency and trust level of claims.

### C. The Operator Interface (Dashboard)
*   **Mission Control:** Real-time telemetry monitoring node sync, encryption depth, and active project stability.
*   **System Configuration:** Administrative controls for investigative identity masking, failsafe purges, and security protocols.
*   **Research Queue:** The ingestion pipeline where AI agents and human analysts collaborate on incoming signals.

---

## 03 — Design System: "Controlled Menace"
*   **Canvas:** Foundations in deep black (#050505) to emphasize high-contrast data layers.
*   **Palette:** 
    *   **Primary Action (Orange):** #ff5f1f — Used for CTAs, urgency, and active telemetry.
    *   **Verification (Lime):** #a3e935 — Used for trust signals, verified status, and system health.
    *   **Warning (Amber/Rust):** Semantic signals for breaches and critical failures.
*   **Typography:**
    *   **Headline:** Space Grotesk (Authority, precision).
    *   **Body:** Inter (Legibility, clarity).
    *   **Data/Code:** JetBrains Mono (Technical grounding, forensic detail).
*   **Atmosphere:** Glass surfaces with 4% opacity, high-fidelity WebGL shaders for hero backgrounds, and restrained motion.

---

## 04 — Agentic Workflow (Human-in-the-Loop)
The platform is powered by a 6-agent editorial pipeline:
1.  **Research Agent:** Aggregates raw signals.
2.  **Fact Check Agent:** Annotates claims with verification status.
3.  **Writing Agent:** Produces draft Case Files.
4.  **SEO Agent:** Optimizes for intelligence discoverability.
5.  **Architect Agent:** Identifies deployment requirements.
6.  **Reviewer Agent:** Audits against the platform constitution.
*   **Rule:** No agent has autonomous publish rights. Every public change requires a human approval gate.

---

## 05 — Technical Specifications
*   **Frontend:** Next.js / React / Tailwind CSS / shadcn.
*   **CMS:** WordPress + Hello Elementor (FSE) + ACF Pro (17 Custom Post Types).
*   **Visuals:** SVG icon library, WebGL procedural shaders, and high-fidelity 3D isometric neural visualizations.
*   **Compliance:** WCAG 2.1 AA contrast, mobile-first responsiveness (390px/768px/1280px), and secure SSL/AES-256 encryption standards.
